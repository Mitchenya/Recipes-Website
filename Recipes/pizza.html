Ingredients:

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread flour
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Water

Preparation steps:

  1. Mix water, sugar and yeast in a bowl
  2. Add flour, oil and salt
  3. Mix until you have the correct dough texture
  4. Roll out your pizza dough
  5. Let the dough come to room temperature
  6. Prepare your surface with oil
  7. Shape your pizza dough
  8. Apply your desired toppings
  9. Place into the oven to bake
